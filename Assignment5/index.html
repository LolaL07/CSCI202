Photo Scavenger Hunt

How it works

This is a picture scavenger hunt. Your goal is to find where each of the photos below have been taken and recreate them. The ones you see below are zoomed in versions of the originals. Each image contains or was taken at a landmark somewhere within the vicinity of the lawn surrounding the paths between the Fairhaven buildings and the Academic Instructional Center building. When you think you've found it, press reveal to see where the original photos were taken.
